Breakfast Signatures

Ghee Sakkara Pongal

Our most requested starter. Rich Moong dal cooked with raw rice, melted A2 Ghee, and topped with fried cashews and raisins.

Medhu Vada (Crispy & Fluffy)

Traditionally hand-ground Urad dal batter, fried to golden perfection. Served with our famous coconut and ginger chutneys.

Mini Idli Sambar

Button-sized soft idlis submerged in our thick, aromatic Madurai-style vegetable sambar topped with a dollop of ghee.

Filter Coffee Service

Strong, aromatic Kumbakonam-style filter coffee served in traditional brass davaras for an authentic experience.

Lunch Signatures

Traditional South Indian Thali

A grand leaf service featuring 21 items including specialty Poriyal, Avial, Kootu, Vathal Kuzhambu, and fresh Curd.

Hyderabad Biryani

Hyderabadi Biryani is a world-renowned culinary masterpiece featuring fragrant long-grain basmati rice and marinated vegetables or protein slow-cooked in layers using the traditional "dum" method to lock in a complex blend of exotic spices.

Elaneer Payasam

Our unique dessert made with fresh tender coconut water, pulp, and thick cardamom-infused milk. Served chilled.

Dinner Signatures

Ghee Roast Dosa (Live Counter)

Paper-thin, crispy dosas made fresh at the counter with pure ghee and served with a variety of 4 spicy chutneys.

Live Ice Cream Counter

Experience the theater of dessert! Watch our chefs roll fresh milk and your choice of seasonal fruits—from Alphonso Mango to Madurai Jasmine-infused cream—on a sub-zero cold plate, served instantly for maximum freshness

Chilli Parotta

Soft parotta chunks simmered in a rich, tangy tomato-onion gravy with capsicum, creating a melt-in-the-mouth texture with every bite.
