Our Journey

Sri Sri Padmavathi Catering was founded with a passion to serve authentic, traditional pure vegetarian food. What started as a small initiative in Madurai has grown into a trusted name with over 15+ years of experience.

We specialize in "Suddha Saiva Samayal" for marriages, Seemantham, and corporate events, ensuring that every plate served carries the warmth of home-cooked flavors.
